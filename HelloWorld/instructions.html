WebVR (Web Virtual Reality) demo
Fly around the world; teleport to any destination.
To enable WebVR: Configure your browser using the instructions at webvr.info
Highest Quality version of the demo

Touch / Mobile

Press and hold to fly forwards

VR Controller

Point in the direction of travel; touch the front/back of the primary botton to fly forwards/backwards:
Click the VR button (bottom right) to enter WebVR mode.

Keyboard

Arrow keys move forward, back, left, right.
[ and ] (or Page Up and Page Down) control altitude.
f to fly
1 to teleport to a new interesting location
2 to teleport to a new random location
