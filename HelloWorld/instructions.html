This is a demonstation of a WebVR (Virtual Reality) web page. You can fly around the world, and find and teleport to any destination.
Click the 'VR' button to enter VR mode.

VR Controller

Point in the direction of travel; use the primary touch button to fly forwards or backwards:

Keyboard

Use arrow keys to move forward, back, left, right.
Use [ and ] keys to control altitude.
Use f to keep flying.
